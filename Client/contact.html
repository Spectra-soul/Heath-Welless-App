Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or using our online booking system in the dashboard.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept various insurance plans for certain services. Please contact us with your insurance details for verification.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical records or information about your health history.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment with at least 24 hours notice without any cancellation fee.
